# Pattern Analysis: @serhant

Profile: https://www.instagram.com/serhant/
Analyzed: 2026-04-13
Sample size: 3 carousel posts

---

## 1. Caption Structure

**Template observed:**
```
[Address, City, State] • [Price or Monthly Rent]

–

[Narrative description — 3-5 sentences covering: setting, size, style, standout features, outdoor spaces]

–

For more information on this listing click the link in our bio 🏡

–

[Address] • [N Beds] • [N Full Baths] • [N Half Bath] • [Price]

–

Repped By [Full Name] (@handle) [Full Name] (@handle) [Team Name] (@handle)
```

**Key structural traits:**
- Address-first opening (bold, prominent)
- Clean "–" line separators between every block
- Description block is dense but readable (3-5 sentences)
- Specs are repeated at the bottom for easy scanning
- Always credits agent(s) by full name + handle
- Emoji: only 🏡 once, at bio CTA

---

## 2. Slide Visual Patterns

- **Slide 1 always has a text overlay** with: "Just Listed" or "Featured" label + address + city/state + ask price
- Slide 1 uses exterior photo (curb appeal / architectural shot)
- Slides 2–3: interior or outdoor spaces, no visible text overlay
- Carousel count: 3 slides per post (consistent)

---

## 3. Hooks & Value Propositions

- Location first: address as headline signals transparency and confidence
- "Just Listed" / "Featured" label on slide 1 creates urgency/recency
- Narrative focuses on lifestyle: "effortless year-round living", "sun-filled interiors"
- Specs block at end (beds, baths, sqft, price) enables quick decision-making

---

## 5. Tone & Language

- Formal but approachable American English
- Adjective-heavy: "impeccably maintained", "effortless", "gracious", "inviting"
- Avoids exclamation marks in description body
- One emoji max (🏡) used functionally, not decoratively

---

## 6. Applicable Lessons for @meular.jp

- **Address-first format** builds credibility and transparency — adapt to Japanese addresses (area/city/ward format since full addresses may not be appropriate)
- **"–" separators** make dense captions scannable — use in Portuguese captions
- **Specs summary at bottom** (beds, baths, sqft/㎡, price) is essential for real estate posts
- **Agent credit** → adapt to "Fale com nosso corretor: @meular.jp ou via WhatsApp"
- **3-slide minimum** for carousels: exterior → interior highlight → key feature/outdoor
- **Label on slide 1** (e.g., "À Venda" / "Novo Anúncio") anchors the content format
