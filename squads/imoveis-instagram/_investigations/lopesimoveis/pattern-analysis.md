## 1. Caption Structure

**Template A — Property Launch:**
```
[Discovery hook: "procure no google pelo código [CODE]" OR aspirational headline] ✨

[Paragraph 1: location/lifestyle value proposition — 2-3 sentences]

[Paragraph 2: product details — unit count, floor plans, differentiators]

[Quote block: "Torre única, poucas unidades e plantas amplas..." — adds editorial credibility]

[Paragraph 3: amenities and lifestyle features]

👉 [Action CTA: "Deslize o carrossel e descubra cada detalhe..."]
📲 [Contact CTA: "Agende sua visita com um de nossos Corretores Associados Lopes."]
```

**Template B — Educational Content:**
```
[Myth-busting headline: "Comprar o primeiro imóvel não é impossível, só falta a informação certa"]

[Paragraph: context and empathy — why the myth exists]

[Engagement question: "Me conta: qual desses mitos você já acreditou?"]

[Soft CTA: "Se quiser entender seu perfil... fala com a Lopes. A gente te ajuda em todo o processo ❤️"]
```

## 5. Tone & Language

- Brazilian Portuguese — warm, aspirational, real estate marketing register
- Pairs formal aspirational language with occasional direct/familiar tone ("A gente te ajuda")
- Emoji usage: strategic — ✨ for luxury/aspiration, 👉 for action, 📲 for contact, ❤️ for warmth
- Hashtags: moderate use, 4-5 tags on educational content
- No hashtags on launch posts (common for premium real estate content in Brazil)

---

## 6. Applicable Lessons for @meular.jp

- **"👉 Deslize o carrossel" CTA** is effective and natural in Brazilian Portuguese — include in every carousel post
- **📲 contact CTA** should reference WhatsApp (primary channel for Brazil/Latin community): "📲 Fale com a gente pelo WhatsApp"
- **Aspirational language formula** applies perfectly: sofisticação, exclusividade, localização → adapt to Japanese context: "tranquilidade", "praticidade", "novo começo no Japão"
- **Quote-style block** for property highlights builds credibility — consider a one-liner lifestyle quote per property
- **Educational content** (myths, tips, how-to-buy-in-Japan series) would work well for @meular.jp to build trust with first-time buyers
- **Google search code** idea → adapt to: "Acesse meular.jp e busque pelo código [X]" for lead tracking
- **No hashtags on premium listings** (or very few) is common practice for this brand tier
