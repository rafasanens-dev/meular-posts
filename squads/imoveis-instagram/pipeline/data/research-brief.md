## Key Market Insights

### Japan-Specific Filters (non-negotiable)
1. **Walking distance to station** — The #1 property search signal in Japan. Always include, always in minutes on foot.
2. **Pet allowed / Pet OK** — Majority of Japanese properties prohibit pets. When a property allows pets, it's a major differentiator.
3. **No key money / No deposit** — Removes a major friction point for foreign buyers. Highlight when applicable.
4. **Renovation status ("recém reformado")** — The Meu Lar JP differentiator. Renovation as a service is the brand's unique positioning.

### Audience Profile (Meu Lar JP)
- Primary: Brazilians living in Japan (especially Aichi-ken region — Nagoya, Toyota, Hamamatsu)
- Secondary: Other Spanish/Portuguese-speaking foreigners in Japan
- Intent: Long-term residents buying their first property, existing clients upgrading
- Platform behavior: Instagram for discovery, WhatsApp for conversion
- Pain points: Language barrier with Japanese real estate, distrust of key money system, renovation complexity

### Competitive Positioning
- @tokyohousing.group targets English-speaking foreigners → Meu Lar JP has less competition in the Portuguese segment
- @lopesimoveis has the same audience type but in Brazil → import their copy structure, adapt for Japan context
- Japanese real estate accounts are mostly in Japanese → Portuguese content is genuinely scarce for this segment

## Vocabulary Extracted from Profiles

### Terms that perform well
- "a poucos minutos da estação" / "X min a pé da estação Y"
- "Pet OK" (exactly this — universal signal in Japan market)
- "deslize para ver as fotos" / "deslize o carrossel"
- "fale com a gente pelo WhatsApp"
- "recém reformado" / "reforma inclusa" (Meu Lar JP specific)
- "ideal para quem quer" + [specific lifestyle benefit]
- "espaço aberto com potencial para área gourmet"

### Terms that underperform
- "incrível" / "imperdível" / "sensacional" — generic, creates skepticism
- "imóvel dos sonhos" — universal cliché, no differentiation
- "oportunidade única" — false scarcity signal
- "confira" / "olha só" — weak scroll-stoppers
- English real estate jargon ("listing", "property", "amenities") — wrong register for this audience

## Content Mix Recommendation
- **3x/week:** Property listing carousel (core business)
- **1x/week:** Educational content (myths about buying in Japan, financing, key money explained)
- **1x/2 weeks:** Renovation showcase (before/after — reinforces Meu Lar JP's unique value)

---

## Anti-Patterns from Investigation
1. No station proximity → immediate trust loss with Japan audience
2. Caption without hook in first 125 chars → hidden behind "...ver mais"
3. No WhatsApp CTA → conversion dead end
4. Hashtag spam (>8 tags) → looks desperate, hurts reach
5. Generic copy that applies to any property → no differentiation, no memorability
