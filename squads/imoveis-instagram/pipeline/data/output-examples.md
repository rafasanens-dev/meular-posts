## Quality Bar Reference

Both examples demonstrate:
1. Station walking distance in BOTH slide 1 AND caption
2. Price visible in slide 1 (card) AND caption spec block
3. CTA WhatsApp with wa.me link — not just "entre em contato"
4. Slide count under 10 (8 and 6 respectively)
5. Room descriptions are benefit-oriented (not just "sala com janelas")
6. Hook under 125 chars and specific (not generic)
